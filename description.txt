Terraria but ...

NPC's hp, damage is randomize
NPC hitting player will reduce player max life

Weapon have 0.25% chance to break
Tool have 0.01% chance to break
Weapon will have controlled randomize stats
Killing enemy will randomly boost stats slightly
Hitting a enemy will have rare chance to drop random weapon accordingly to progression
Enemy have randomize damage resistance against certain damage type

On getting hit :
+ You have 1% chance to get teleport randomly
+ Your max hp is reduced from the range of 1 to 10
+ Getting hit have a chance to drop the current weapon you are holding and a rarer chance to drop everything
+ Death will randomize your stats to a extend

Tile have 50% to not drop anything and 50% to not be mined at all
Pot have chaotic effect added into them

This mod :
- Isn't meant to be balanced, it is meant to be a "experience" so don't expect it to be very playable.
- Won't be maintain unless tmod drop a new update that affect this mod code, then I will do smth about it
